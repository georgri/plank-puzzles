Planks Puzzles playground (ThinkFun River Crossing)

Clickmazes.com

Plank puzzle concept and maze designs were originally conceived by Andrea Gilbert.

This app is a hack of this code by brambleshadow4.

All puzzles here are recreated from this pdf: ThinkFun River Crossing instructions

To play: use your mouse to pick up and drop the planks to navigate across the screen from red to green circle. You can only pick up planks connected to where you are (highlighted in yellow), and you can only place planks into gaps that are the same size.
